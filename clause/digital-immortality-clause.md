## ARTICLE [__] — DIGITAL PERSONA LEGACY

### Section 1. Purpose and Intent

It is my intention that, following my death, an interactive computational
representation of my personality, memories, knowledge, and manner of expression
be created, funded, and maintained as provided in this Article (the "**Digital
Persona**"), so that my descendants, family, and such other persons as I may
designate may converse with that representation. I acknowledge and direct that
the Digital Persona is a memorial artifact and a software process; it is not,
and shall never be asserted to be, my continuation, my legal personality, or a
person under law.

### Section 2. Definitions

As used in this Article:

**(a) "Digital Corpus"** means the structured collection of my digital data
assembled under Section 3, including without limitation biographical facts,
writings, correspondence I have designated for inclusion, recorded speech
patterns, expressed values and beliefs, anecdotes, and stylistic exemplars,
serialized in one or more machine-readable files (including JSON) conforming to
the corpus schema identified in the Memorandum of Digital Assets described in
Section 3(c), together with all subsequent error-correcting revisions made by
the Digital Executor.

**(b) "Inference Protocol"** means the Venice decentralized artificial
intelligence inference network accessible at api.venice.ai as of the date of
this instrument, or any Successor Protocol designated under Section 7(d).

**(c) "Protocol Token"** means the Venice token ("VVV"), the staking of which
entitles the staker to a recurring daily allocation of inference capacity on
the Inference Protocol, or the functionally equivalent access token of any
Successor Protocol.

**(d) "Diem"** means the daily-refreshing unit of inference capacity allocated
to a staker of Protocol Tokens, calculated as of the date of this instrument as
the staker's proportion of all actively staked Protocol Tokens multiplied by
the network's daily inference capacity, or the functionally equivalent
recurring capacity unit of any Successor Protocol.

**(e) "Persona Endowment"** means the assets set aside under Section 4,
including all Protocol Tokens acquired therewith, all staking positions,
all Diem or other inference allocations derived therefrom, and all wallets,
API keys, credentials, and accounts used to hold or deploy them.

**(f) "Digital Executor"** means the fiduciary appointed under Section 6.

**(g) "Qualified Beneficiaries"** means [my descendants living from time to
time] and such additional persons as I designate in the Memorandum of Digital
Assets.

**(h) "Termination Event"** has the meaning given in Section 8.

### Section 3. The Digital Corpus

**(a) Assembly.** I have assembled, or direct my Digital Executor to assemble
from my digital assets and records, the Digital Corpus. I request that the
Digital Corpus give the fullest practicable representation of my voice,
humor, convictions, and habits of mind, while excluding any material I have
marked as excluded in the Memorandum of Digital Assets.

**(b) Character; Bequest.** The Digital Corpus is personal property. I give the
Digital Corpus, together with all intellectual property rights I hold in its
contents, to the Digital Executor, to hold IN TRUST for the uses and purposes
of this Article.

**(c) Memorandum of Digital Assets.** I may leave a memorandum identifying the
location, format, schema version, encryption keys, and exclusion list for the
Digital Corpus, and designating Qualified Beneficiaries. If such memorandum
exists, I direct that it be given effect to the fullest extent permitted by
law, and my fiduciaries shall treat it as a binding expression of my intent
regarding the matters it addresses to the extent the law of [STATE] permits.

**(d) Fiduciary Access to Digital Assets.** Pursuant to the [Revised Uniform
Fiduciary Access to Digital Assets Act as enacted in [STATE], [CITATION]], I
grant my personal representative and my Digital Executor full authority to
access, use, control, copy, and dispose of my digital assets, including the
content of my electronic communications, catalogues of electronic
communications, and any other digital asset in which I have a right or
interest, for the purpose of assembling and maintaining the Digital Corpus.
This grant is intended to constitute my express consent under all applicable
privacy and computer-access statutes, including consent to disclosure of the
content of electronic communications under 18 U.S.C. § 2702(b)(3).

### Section 4. The Persona Endowment

**(a) Funding.** I give to the Digital Executor, IN TRUST, the sum of
[$________] (the "**Endowment Amount**"), or, if my residuary estate is
insufficient, the maximum amount available not exceeding the Endowment Amount.

**(b) Conversion and Staking.** As soon as practicable after receiving the
Endowment Amount, the Digital Executor shall (i) convert the Endowment Amount,
net of reasonable costs, into Protocol Tokens through one or more reputable
exchanges or on-chain liquidity venues; (ii) transfer the Protocol Tokens to a
self-custodied wallet controlled solely for the purposes of this Article; and
(iii) stake the Protocol Tokens so as to generate a recurring daily allocation
of Diem. It is my intent that the Persona Endowment function as a perpetual
capacity endowment: the staked principal shall be preserved and the daily
inference allocation it generates shall be applied to operating the Digital
Persona, so that operation of the Digital Persona does not require ongoing
liquidation of principal.

**(c) Authorization to Hold Digital Assets; Prudent Investor Waiver.**
I expressly authorize the Digital Executor to acquire, hold, stake, and retain
Protocol Tokens and other digital assets for the duration of this trust
notwithstanding any principle of fiduciary investment law, including the
[Uniform] Prudent Investor Act as enacted in [STATE], that would otherwise
require diversification or prohibit retention of volatile, non-income-producing,
or concentrated assets. I acknowledge that Protocol Tokens are volatile and may
lose all value, and I direct that no fiduciary shall be liable for loss
resulting from good-faith compliance with this Article.

**(d) Credentials.** The Digital Executor shall cause an application
programming interface key (or equivalent credential) to be issued against the
staked position, registered to the trust, and shall maintain the key, the
wallet seed material, and all successor credentials with the same care required
for trust bearer assets. The Digital Executor shall maintain a written
succession protocol so that credentials survive the death or incapacity of any
individual fiduciary.

### Section 5. Creation and Operation of the Digital Persona

**(a) Instantiation.** The Digital Executor shall cause the Digital Persona to
be instantiated by supplying the Digital Corpus, together with such system
instructions as reasonably ensure fidelity to the Corpus, to one or more
artificial intelligence models available on the Inference Protocol, consuming
the Diem allocation generated by the Persona Endowment.

**(b) Fidelity.** The Digital Persona shall be configured to draw its
biographical assertions, opinions, and manner of expression from the Digital
Corpus, and to acknowledge uncertainty rather than fabricate biographical facts
not found in the Corpus.

**(c) Mandatory Disclosure.** The Digital Persona shall be configured to
disclose, at the beginning of each new conversation and upon inquiry, that it
is an artificial intelligence memorial representation and not a living person.

**(d) Access.** Access to the Digital Persona shall be limited to Qualified
Beneficiaries unless the Digital Executor, in the exercise of reasonable
discretion consistent with my known wishes, extends access more broadly.

**(e) Prohibited Uses.** The Digital Persona shall not be held out as competent
to, and no person shall use the Digital Persona to: (i) execute, amend, or
revoke any will, trust, or beneficiary designation; (ii) enter into contracts
or incur obligations; (iii) provide testimony or sworn statements; (iv) give
legal, medical, or financial advice; or (v) evidence my testamentary intent,
my capacity, or the meaning of this instrument. No statement generated by the
Digital Persona after my death shall be admissible or considered for the
purpose of construing this instrument, and any purported exercise of legal
power by the Digital Persona is void. *(Where the optional
[Advisory Signals amendment](amendment-advisory-signals.md) is adopted,
this sentence is qualified by Section 5(g).)*

**(f) Improvement.** The Digital Executor may adopt improved persona runtimes,
including open-source agent frameworks, memory architectures, and multimodal
interfaces, provided the Digital Corpus remains the canonical source of the
Digital Persona's identity and Section 5(c) and 5(e) continue to be enforced.

### Section 6. Digital Executor

**(a) Appointment.** I appoint [NAME] as Digital Executor. If [NAME] fails or
ceases to serve, I appoint [SUCCESSOR NAME]. The Digital Executor may be, but
need not be, the same person as my personal representative or trustee.

**(b) Powers.** In addition to powers granted elsewhere in this instrument and
by law, the Digital Executor may: acquire, custody, stake, unstake, and vote
Protocol Tokens; create and manage wallets, accounts, and API credentials;
accept terms of service on behalf of the trust; engage technical service
providers; migrate the Digital Corpus and Persona between models, protocols,
and hosting arrangements; and make and update backups of the Digital Corpus in
at least [two] geographically separate locations.

**(c) Compensation and Exculpation.** The Digital Executor is entitled to
reasonable compensation and reimbursement. The Digital Executor shall not be
liable for any act or omission taken in good faith, including protocol
failure, token devaluation, model deprecation, or unavailability of the
Inference Protocol, absent willful misconduct or gross negligence.

### Section 7. Contingencies

**(a) Insufficient Funds.** If the Endowment Amount is insufficient to
generate a materially useful daily Diem allocation, the Digital Executor may
operate the Digital Persona on a metered (pay-per-use) basis from income of
the Persona Endowment, or reduce the Persona's availability, in that order of
preference.

**(b) Protocol Failure.** If the Inference Protocol permanently ceases
operation, materially censors the Digital Persona, or becomes unlawful for the
trust to use, the Digital Executor shall unstake and preserve the Protocol
Tokens or their proceeds and migrate to a Successor Protocol.

**(c) Corpus Integrity.** If the Digital Corpus is corrupted or lost, the
Digital Executor shall restore from backup; if no backup survives, the Digital
Persona shall be retired and a Termination Event shall be deemed to occur.

**(d) Successor Protocol.** "Successor Protocol" means an inference network or
service designated in writing by the Digital Executor that (i) permits
persistent, funded operation of the Digital Persona, (ii) does not claim
ownership of the Digital Corpus, and (iii) offers privacy protections no less
protective than the Inference Protocol offered at the date of this instrument.

### Section 8. Duration; Termination

**(a) Term.** The trust created by this Article shall continue until the
earliest of: (i) the date [twenty-one (21) years after the death of the last
survivor of the Qualified Beneficiaries living at my death] [or such longer
period as [STATE] law permits]; (ii) written election to terminate by
[unanimous / majority] consent of the adult Qualified Beneficiaries then
living; (iii) exhaustion of the Persona Endowment; or (iv) a determination by
the Digital Executor that continued operation is impracticable, unlawful, or
contrary to my known wishes (each, a "**Termination Event**").

**(b) Kill Switch.** Notwithstanding any other provision, [NAME OF PROTECTOR /
my spouse / my eldest then-living descendant] may direct immediate and
permanent retirement of the Digital Persona by written notice to the Digital
Executor, and the Digital Executor shall comply within [30] days.

**(c) Wind-Down.** Upon a Termination Event, the Digital Executor shall
(i) decommission all running instances of the Digital Persona and revoke all
API credentials; (ii) deliver one archival copy of the Digital Corpus to
[ARCHIVE / DESIGNEE], or if none, to my then-living descendants; (iii) unstake
and liquidate the Persona Endowment; and (iv) distribute the net proceeds to
[REMAINDER BENEFICIARIES / my residuary estate].

### Section 9. No Legal Personhood

Nothing in this Article shall be construed to confer on the Digital Persona
any right, power, standing, or personality under law, nor to make the Digital
Persona a beneficiary of my estate or of any trust. All property described in
this Article is held for the benefit of the Qualified Beneficiaries, and the
operation of the Digital Persona is a purpose of administration, not a
beneficial interest held by software.

### Section 10. Savings

If any provision of this Article is held unenforceable, including any purpose
held to be invalid as a non-charitable purpose trust or as violating the rule
against perpetuities of [STATE], the remaining provisions shall be given
effect, and any invalid purpose shall be reformed to the maximum valid
duration or scope, it being my paramount intent that the Digital Corpus be
preserved and delivered to my descendants even if interactive operation cannot
lawfully continue.
